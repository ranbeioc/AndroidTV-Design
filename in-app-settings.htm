In-app settings

In addition to content browsing, your app may need lightweight settings or management. Keep your settings as minimal as possible. If it is not essential, we recommend not providing it on TV.

Settings placement is recommended as the last row in your Browse View. If your app uses Browse Lane, your Settings content should be reflected in the lane.

In-app settings should appear in the Settings panel.

An in-app setting should only be represented as a title, not an action, in the Browse Lane.
